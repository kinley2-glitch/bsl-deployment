How to Use?

  1. Learn a sign from Japanese Sign Language by picking a video from the menu.
  2. Record yourself doing one of the signs.
  3. See the ML model guess what you are signing.

Tips

  1. Make sure you are in a well lit area.
  2. Make sure your entire upper torso, face, arms, and hands are visible to the camera.
  3. Make sure you are the only one visible on camera.
